Landscaping (6 Month)

Design and maintain beautiful gardens

Course Summary

This 6-month Landscaping programme covers plant selection, garden layout, and design aesthetics. Learners explore indigenous and exotic plants, fixed structures like fountains or benches, and balancing greenery for both beauty and sustainability.
